The Begin

It all started a cold afternoon of november, 2019. The three friends met the two co-players of their former band and went for their first beer. That made the job, they agreed that they would agitate the waters of metal scene with their blasting beats. Two shredding guitars, a sharp-knife bass, maniac drum beats and vocals that even the god of thunder would be jealous of.

In the meanwhile

Life was going well for Undervault, music was flowing and the beer was not ending. Until the great time for their singer came and had to move to Scotland to extend his musical knowledge. This was a great loss for the band that only one person could fill, Sofia Kostoglou! Her vocal range ripped their ears apart while embracing themselves. The road was open for new arrangements and composicial oasis!
